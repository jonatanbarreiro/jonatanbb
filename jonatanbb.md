# jonatanbb.xyz

## Concept & purpose

This was supposed to be a personal site in the more traditional sense.
To serve job and practical-life purposes, and maybe to hold stuff that doesn't belong in the book, that is not so personal.
More like the public-facing side of me, for all audiences.

One page is a gallery for a more professional, LinkedIn-type profile — but broader than that in its concept.

I don't have a job right now, and LinkedIn is doing me no favors.
I wouldn't pollute firecoat.me with a section acting as a CV and projects-portfolio, so I decided to build a dedicated website.

jonatanbb.xyz can present the project as what it technically is: an AI project centered around my persona, or at least some representation of it.

## Name

I settled on jonatanbb.xyz: my name, plus my surname initials, plus the .xyz extension, which I read as "cajón desastre" — an aggregation of stuff.

## Design & aesthetics

Bright and professional.
Portraying the day sky, with the sun.
Light coming from the sky.

A photo of my face most likely makes sense here.

Maybe the title page should just include my name and my qb key in bb form (my surnames' first letters), maybe a photo, or a depiction of my face in some way.
Links to social media, and a context message to welcome the user, present the castle (with less symbolism on this page than on firecoat.me, as a way of saying we are more aware, in some sense, at night), and whatever else I want to say there.
Maybe some in-a-nutshell instructions to navigate the page.

## Content & sections

In this page I intend to put anything I wouldn't mind a future employer seeing: diplomas, titles, achievements, a projects overview (sort of like a dev portfolio), maybe some photos of my trip to London, as well as links to my social networks.

Gallery/portfolio section of projects and adventures.

Photo gallery section.

Every piece of content tied to some label or section.

About me.

I could use jonatanbb.xyz as a container for writings related to my life — those passages of the book anchored more in facts of my life.

When I write, I should use the first person and have a destination in mind when I explain my worldviews, so the content is easily adaptable to, or at least includable in, book format.

Will be built as a blog, or around it rather.

## Logo

Both my surnames start with 'b'; the qb key appears here in bb form.

The most prominent primitive of firecoat.me is the fire, which can be signified with a bonfire or flame, which 'db' kind of resembles — one b turned sideways.
The qb form can be made to resemble the Avicii logo if the b's are cut sharp, with a triangle shape.
Avicii lyrics often talk about renewal and fire, and share a lot of conceptual overlap with the theme of firecoat.me; I endured hard times in my life with his music.
Every year on the date of his death (20 April), the logo should be shown in "Avicii form", just for the day.

bb is a square, but also a ying-yang, but also a flame.

The logo is a cute tie for both websites; it should be a nice tie.
