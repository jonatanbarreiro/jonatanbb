% The design is "the plumbing": the portrait's gold frame at the top left; from its
% lower-left corner the main pipe runs down the margin; every section head is a
% KNOT —the site's b rotated 225°, bar crossing the pipe toward the name, belly
% looping under, long stick curving 45° to carry the pipe on down. At skills the
% stem is removed and the bar points at the name with the site's hook; the pipe
% ends. The frame's corners are dressed: half-scale knotty turns at the top left
% (vertical on top) and bottom right (horizontal on top); at the top right the top
% side runs on into a salient stick —hook down, pointing at the middle of the
% name's 'o'— with the right side passing under it; at the bottom left the bottom
% side passes under the main pipe. Every crossing is articulated by a hairline
% SEAM —the under-pipe stops just short of the pipe above, the knot-diagram cue
% for passing beneath. In the knots the pipe rides the belly's dive x through the
% whole knot, so it crosses the bar right where it re-emerges below.
