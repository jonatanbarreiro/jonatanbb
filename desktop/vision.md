## Story & vision

A living record of how the source of **jonatanbb.xyz** is built, iteration by
iteration, with Claude Code. The site is developed under `sources/version0/`,
where each `iterationN/` folder is one development step toward **version0** —
each carrying its own `interaction_*` files that document the turn(s) behind it.
Whole sessions are logged verbatim under `interactions/`. The repo and the site
mirror each other: when the site declares version0, so does the repo.
